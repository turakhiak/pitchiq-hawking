# SaaS & Technology Industry Benchmarks

## Growth Metrics

### Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) Growth
- **Median Growth Rate**: 30% (2024 data from SaaS Capital)
- **Top Quartile**: 50%+ annual growth
- **Seed Stage**: 200-300% year-over-year growth
- **Series A**: 100-200% YoY growth
- **Series B+**: 50-100% YoY growth

### Revenue Multiples
- **Valuation/Revenue**: 5-15x for high-growth SaaS
- **Early Stage (Seed/A)**: 10-20x ARR
- **Growth Stage (B/C)**: 8-12x ARR
- **Late Stage**: 5-8x ARR

## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **B2B SaaS Average**: $1.18 per $1 of new ARR
- **CAC Payback Period**: 5-12 months (median: 9 months)
- **Sales & Marketing % of Revenue**: 40-50% for growth stage
- **Efficient Growth**: 6x revenue return per dollar spent on sales/marketing

### Lifetime Value (LTV)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Minimum 3:1 (healthy: 5:1+)
- **Average Customer Lifetime**: 3-5 years
- **Annual Contract Value (ACV)**:
  - SMB: $1,000 - $10,000
  - Mid-market: $10,000 - $100,000
  - Enterprise: $100,000+

### Churn Rate
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: 100-130% (best-in-class: 130%+)
- **Logo Churn**: <5% annually for enterprise, <10% for SMB
- **Gross Churn**: 5-7% annually

## Financial Health

### Rule of 40
- **Definition**: Growth Rate + Profit Margin
- **Target**: >40% indicates healthy balance
- **High Growth**: Can sacrifice profitability temporarily

### Burn Multiple
- **Definition**: Net Burn / Net New ARR
- **Excellent**: <1x
- **Good**: 1-1.5x
- **Needs Improvement**: >2x

### Gross Margin
- **Target**: 70-80%+
- **Freemium Products**: 60-70%
- **Enterprise SaaS**: 75-85%

## Operating Metrics

### Sales Efficiency
- **Magic Number**: >0.75 (excellent: >1.0)
- **Formula**: New ARR / Sales & Marketing Spend
- **Top Performers**: Generate $1.50+ in revenue per $1 spent

### Team Structure (% of headcount)
- **Engineering**: 30-40%
- **Sales & Marketing**: 25-35%
- **Customer Success**: 10-15%
- **G&A**: 10-15%

## Funding Benchmarks

### Typical Raise Amounts (2024)
- **Pre-Seed**: $500K - $2M
- **Seed**: $2M - $5M
- **Series A**: $8M - $15M
- **Series B**: $20M - $40M
- **Series C+**: $50M+

### Runway Expectations
- **Post-Raise Runway**: 18-24 months
- **Burn Rate**: Varies by stage, but typically $100K-$500K/month for Series A

## Technology Stack Indicators

### Infrastructure Spend
- **% of Revenue**: 15-25% (cloud infrastructure, tools)
- **Per-Employee Tech Spend**: $5,000-$10,000/year

### Development Cycle
- **Release Frequency**: Weekly to bi-weekly deployments
- **Time to Market**: 3-6 months for major features

---
*Sources: SaaS Capital 2024 Survey, Benchmarkit, Lighter Capital B2B Insights, Capchase SaaS Benchmark Report 2024*
