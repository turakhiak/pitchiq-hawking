# E-commerce & Retail Industry Benchmarks

## Conversion Metrics

### Cart & Checkout
- **Average Conversion Rate**: 2-3% overall
  - Desktop: 3-4%
  - Mobile: 1-2%
  - Tablet: 3-3.5%
- **Add-to-Cart Rate**: 8-12%
- **Cart Abandonment Rate**: 68-70%
- **Checkout Abandonment**: 25-30% (of those who start checkout)

### Browse-to-Buy Optimization
- **Product Page Views to Cart**: 10-15%
- **Time on Site**: 2-4 minutes average
- **Pages per Session**: 4-6 pages
- **Bounce Rate**: 40-50%

## Revenue Metrics

### Gross Merchandise Volume (GMV)
- **Strong Growth**: 30-50% YoY for early-stage
- **Mature Marketplaces**: 15-25% YoY
- **Take Rate**: 10-30% depending on model
  - Marketplace: 10-20%
  - Fullfillment by Merchant: 8-12%
  - Full Service: 20-30%

### Average Order Value (AOV)
- **Fashion**: $50-$100
- **Electronics**: $200-$500
- **Home & Garden**: $75-$150
- **Luxury**: $500-$2,000

### Customer Economics
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: $10-$50 (varies widely)
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: $100-$500 for most categories
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Minimum 3:1
- **CAC Payback Period**: 6-12 months

## Customer Behavior

### Repeat Purchase Rate
- **First Purchase to Second**: 20-30%
- **Active Customers (3+ purchases/year)**: 15-25%
- **VIP Customers (10+ purchases/year)**: 5-10% (generate 30-40% of revenue)

### Retention
- **30-day Retention**: 20-30%
- **90-day Retention**: 10-20%
- **12-month Retention**: 5-15%
- **Annual Churn**: 60-80%

### Session Metrics
- **Return Visitor Rate**: 30-40%
- **New vs. Returning**: 60-70% new, 30-40% returning
- **Average Session Duration**: 2-5 minutes

## Operational Metrics

### Fulfillment
- **Order Processing Time**: 1-2 days
- **Shipping Time**: 2-7 days (standard)
- **On-Time Delivery**: 95%+
- **Returns Rate**: 20-30% (fashion), 5-10% (electronics)
- **Return Processing Time**: 7-14 days

### Inventory
- **Inventory Turnover**: 4-8x annually
- **Out-of-Stock Rate**: <5% target
- **Overstock**: <10% of inventory

## Financial Health

### Margins
- **Gross Margin**: 40-60% for direct-to-consumer
  - Apparel: 50-60%
  - Electronics: 15-30%
  - Marketplace: 70-80% (low COGS)
- **Operating Margin**: 0-10% (often negative for growth stage)
- **EBITDA Margin**: 5-15% for profitable companies

### Unit Economics
- **Contribution Margin**: 20-30% target
- **Customer Service Cost**: $5-$10 per order
- **Marketing Spend**: 10-20% of GMV

## Traffic & Marketing

### Channel Mix
- **Direct Traffic**: 20-30%
- **Organic Search**: 30-40%
- **Paid Search**: 15-25%
- **Social Media**: 10-20%
- **Email**: 10-15%

### Marketing Efficiency
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**: 3:1 minimum (4-5:1 healthy)
- **Cost per Click (CPC)**: $0.50-$3 (varies by industry)
- **Email Open Rate**: 15-25%
- **Email Click Rate**: 2-5%

## Mobile Commerce

### Mobile Performance
- **Mobile Traffic**: 60-70% of all traffic
- **Mobile Conversion**: 1-2% (lower than desktop)
- **Mobile AOV**: 20-30% lower than desktop
- **App vs. Mobile Web**: App users convert 3x higher

## Funding Benchmarks

### Typical Raise Amounts (2024)
- **Seed**: $1M - $5M
- **Series A**: $5M - $15M
- **Series B**: $15M - $40M
- **Series C+**: $50M+

### Runway
- **Post-Raise Runway**: 18-24 months
- **Burn Rate**: Highly variable ($50K - $2M/month)

## Market Growth

### E-commerce Penetration
- **Global E-commerce CAGR**: 10-15%
- **US E-commerce % of Retail**: 15-20%
- **Emerging Markets Growth**: 20-30% CAGR

### Category Growth Rates
- **Fashion & Apparel**: 8-10%
- **Electronics**: 6-8%
- **Grocery**: 15-20%
- **Luxury**: 10-12%

---
*Sources: Statista E-commerce Reports, Shopify Commerce Trends, eMarketer Retail Data, Baymard Institute Cart Abandonment Research*
