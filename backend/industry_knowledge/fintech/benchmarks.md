# FinTech & Digital Payments Industry Benchmarks

## Transaction Metrics

### Payment Success Rate
- **Industry Standard**: 99.9% or better
- **Authorization Rate**: 85-95%
- **Acceptance Rate**: 90-98%
- **Decline Rate**: 5-10% (varies by industry)

### Processing Performance
- **Platform Uptime**: 99.99% (four nines)
- **Processing Speed**: <2 seconds average
- **Time to Settlement**: 2-5 business days (varies by payment method)

## Revenue Metrics

### Total Payment Volume (TPV)
- **Strong Growth**: 15-20% month-over-month
- **Mature Companies**: 5-10% MoM growth
- **Transaction Volume**: Varies widely by use case

### Take Rate (Revenue Margin)
- **Typical Range**: 0.5-2.5% of TPV
- **Credit Cards**: 1.5-3.5%
- **ACH/Bank Transfer**: 0.5-1%
- **Digital Wallets**: 1-2%
- **Benchmark**: 1.05% (industry average)

### Revenue per User (RPU)
- **Consumer Fintech**: $20-$100/month
- **B2B Payments**: $500-$5,000/month
- **Enterprise**: $10,000+/month

## User Metrics

### User Acquisition
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Varies by segment
  - Consumer: $50-$200
  - SMB: $500-$2,000
  - Enterprise: $10,000-$50,000
- **CAC Payback**: 6-18 months

### Engagement
- **Monthly Active Users (MAU)**: Key metric for consumer fintech
  - Example: Cash App has 56M MAU
- **Transaction Frequency**: 10-20 transactions/month for high engagement
- **Activation Rate**: 60-80% of signups complete first transaction

### Retention
- **30-day Retention**: 40-60%
- **90-day Retention**: 20-40%
- **Churn Rate**: 5-10% monthly for consumer, <5% for B2B

## Security & Risk

### Fraud Metrics
- **Fraud Rate**: <0.1% for best-in-class
- **Acceptable Fraud Rate**: 0.5-1%
- **False Positive Rate**: <5% (important for UX)
- **Fraud Detection Time**: <1 second for real-time systems

### Chargebacks
- **Chargeback Rate**: <0.9% (Visa/Mastercard threshold: 1%)
- **Dispute Resolution Time**: 30-45 days average
- **Win Rate**: 20-40% for merchants

### Compliance
- **KYC Verification Success**: 85-95%
- **AML Alert Rate**: 1-5% of transactions flagged
- **Regulatory Compliance Cost**: 10-15% of revenue

## Financial Health

### Margins
- **Gross Margin**: 40-60% for payments, 60-80% for software-enabled fintech
- **EBITDA Margin**: 10-20% for mature companies
- **Operating Margin**: 5-15%

### Efficiency
- **Cost to Serve**: $10-$50 per active customer annually
- **Infrastructure Costs**: 30-40% of revenue for early-stage

## Funding Metrics

### Typical Raise Amounts (2024)
- **Seed**: $3M - $10M (higher than traditional SaaS due to regulatory needs)
- **Series A**: $15M - $30M
- **Series B**: $40M - $80M
- **Series C+**: $100M+

### Capital Requirements
- **Regulatory Capital**: Varies by license (e.g., money transmitter licenses)
- **Runway**: 18-36 months (longer than SaaS due to regulatory timelines)

## Market Benchmarks

### Digital Payments Growth
- **Global Digital Payments Volume**: Growing at 15-20% CAGR
- **Mobile Payments**: 25-30% CAGR
- **Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)**: 20-25% CAGR

### Regional Performance
- **North America**: Mature markets, 10-15% growth
- **Asia-Pacific**: High growth, 25-35% CAGR
- **Europe**: Moderate growth, 15-20% CAGR (PSD2 impact)

---
*Sources: Juniper Research, Grand View Research, The Paypers Global Payments Report, Silicon Valley Bank FinTech Insights*
